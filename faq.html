📋 Booking Process
How do I book a trip with Voyage Experts? +
Simply fill in our enquiry form on the Contact page, or call us directly. Our travel expert will reach out within 2 hours with an initial consultation. You'll receive a customised itinerary within 24 hours — completely free, no obligation.
Can I customise a package I see on your website? +
Absolutely. Every package listed is a starting template. We customise flights, hotels, activities, duration, and group size to match your exact preferences and budget. There's no extra charge for customisation.
How early should I book my holiday? +
For peak season (October–March) and popular destinations like Maldives or Europe, we recommend booking 2–3 months in advance. For off-peak travel, 4–6 weeks notice is usually sufficient. Last-minute bookings (under 2 weeks) are possible but may have limited availability.
Do you handle group and corporate bookings? +
Yes! We specialise in group travel for families, friends, and corporate retreats of 10 to 500+ people. We offer dedicated group coordinators, bulk pricing, and end-to-end logistics management.
💳 Payments & Pricing
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI, bank transfer, credit/debit cards, EMI options (0% EMI on select packages), and net banking. International payments can be processed via wire transfer or PayPal.
What is the deposit amount to confirm a booking? +
A deposit of 25% of the total package value is required to confirm your booking. The remaining balance is due 30 days before departure. For last-minute bookings, full payment is required upfront.
Do you offer a price match guarantee? +
Yes! If you find the same itinerary at a lower price from a IATA-certified agency, we'll match it — or beat it by 5%. Just share the quote within 48 hours of receiving ours.
Are there hidden charges? +
Never. Our quoted price includes everything listed in the itinerary. Any optional add-ons (travel insurance, extra excursions) are clearly itemised. No surprises at checkout or at the destination.
🛂 Visa & Documentation
Do you assist with visa applications? +
Yes — full visa documentation support is included in all international packages. Our team prepares your checklist, reviews documents, and submits applications on your behalf. We have a 98% visa approval rate.
Which documents do I need to provide? +
Typically: valid passport (6 months+ validity), recent passport-size photos, bank statements (last 3–6 months), proof of accommodation, and flight bookings. Your travel consultant will provide a personalised checklist for your specific destination.
How long does visa processing take? +
Processing time varies by country: UAE/Thailand (24–72 hours), Schengen/UK (10–15 working days), USA/Canada (4–8 weeks). We advise starting visa applications as soon as your booking is confirmed.
🔄 Cancellations & Changes
What is the cancellation policy? +
Cancellation charges are based on days before departure: 60+ days — 10% fee; 30–59 days — 25% fee; 15–29 days — 50% fee; under 15 days — 100% non-refundable. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover unexpected cancellations.
Can I change my travel dates after booking? +
Date changes are permitted subject to availability. If changed more than 45 days before departure, a nominal amendment fee of ₹2,500 applies. Within 45 days, airline and hotel penalties may apply. We always work to minimise costs for you.
📞 Travel Support
What support do I get while travelling? +
All travellers get access to our 24/7 emergency helpline, a dedicated WhatsApp support group, and in-destination contacts. For luxury packages, a personal travel concierge is assigned throughout your trip.
Do you offer travel insurance? +
Yes — we partner with leading insurers to offer comprehensive travel insurance covering medical emergencies, trip cancellation, baggage loss, and flight delays. We strongly recommend it for all international travel. Premiums start from ₹799/person/trip.
Are your packages suitable for senior citizens? +
Absolutely. We have specially curated easy-pace itineraries for senior travellers, with wheelchair-accessible hotels, medical support contacts, and slower-paced sightseeing schedules. Just let us know your requirements and we'll tailor everything.
